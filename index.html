Through the Aztec Empire


The Aztec Empire, also known as the Triple Alliance, was formed by three Nahua city-states: Tenochtitlán, Texcoco, and Tlacopan. Together, they controlled the Valley of Mexico from 1428 until they were conquered by Spanish forces and their native allies under Hernán Cortés in 1521.
